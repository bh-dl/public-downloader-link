In order to save the bookmarklet, drag the button onto your browser's bookmarks bar. Save it with whatever name you'd like. Once you go to a public BoundHub video, click the button and it will take you directly to their video host, from which you can do 
File -> Save
, or 
Ctrl + S

Important note: you must let the video play for a second to actually load the video content. You'll be able to use the bookmarklet by simply clicking play and then pausing as soon as it loads on BoundHub.
